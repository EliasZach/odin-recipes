Orange Chicken

Chicken breasts marinated in a citrus orange sauce and spices, cooked to perfection for a sweet and aromatic dish. Perfect to accompany with rice or salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Juice of 2 oranges
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the orange juice, zest, soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the chicken breasts in this mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the breasts for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Serve with rice or salad.
