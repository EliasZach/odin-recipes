Chicken Curry

Chicken cubes cooked in a rich coconut milk curry sauce, an explosion of exotic flavors in every bite. Ideal to serve with rice and enjoy a comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 400 ml of coconut milk
  • Olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat and saut√© the onion and garlic until golden.
  2. Add the chicken cubes and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the curry powder and mix well.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and season with salt. Simmer for about 15 minutes until the chicken is cooked.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
