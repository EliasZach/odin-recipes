Oven-baked chicken with vegetables

Juicy chicken thighs baked with seasonal vegetables, a complete and easy meal to prepare on a single tray. Ideal for a healthy and uncomplicated dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of Herbes de Provence (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 °C (400 °F).
  2. On a baking sheet, place the chicken thighs and surround with the vegetables.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and Herbes de Provence.
  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot.
